Frequently AskedQuestions

What materials should be used for specific building parts?

Bricks or aerated concrete are often used for external walls, while reinforced concrete is typical for load-bearing structures.

What types of building permits are required for this project?

Depending on the project, a building permit, a demolition permit, or a change of use permit may be required.

How long does the construction process take?

The construction time varies depending on the project scope, but a single-family house typically takes 6 to 12 months.

How are moisture and waterproofing issues resolved?

Moisture problems are addressed using appropriate waterproofing techniques and the application of sealants.
